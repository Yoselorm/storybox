The cool air, mountain tops, the up and downhill roads, the vast area of lands and rich culture of the people she loved. At least this was a new start for her since her perfect record of bad relationships and heartbreak, she was an epitome of beauty most would say

The city of Lagos made her sick as it reminded her of the men who were out there seeking to destroy beautuful yet fragile women like her in every aspect of their lives they could. She had been heartbroken too many times thus making her reclusive and an all time emotional wreck it was having a major effect on her life and career and mostly her family.
After undergoing various counseling from the best they could find she jumped back to life and decided it was time for a fresh start. With a Bachelor's degree in insurance she was lucky to be a working staff of one of the best insurance companies there was in the country and asked to be transferred out of Lagos. There she was in the beautiful city of Ibadan starting afresh "Good riddance to bad rubbish" Laura said, as she walked down the familiar road to work .

They were nice and caring to him until God blessed them with their own kids, they forget about him and poured all of their love on their kids. When time came for him to go to the university, he chose to go to Ibadan to study as to get away from them and the love he couldn't get. He had always been an extrovert with an extremely weird personality, kept to himself much and had little friends or "acquantainces" he would say. Never had he got his head around women.
